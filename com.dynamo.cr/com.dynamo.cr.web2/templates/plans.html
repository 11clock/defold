Plans

We charge for Defold by the size of your teams. By team size, we mean the total number of people working on a project. For example, if you get the Small plan you can join projects with one member, or invite one person to your own projects. The fees below are per person and month. You can currently only create a limited amount of projects, but join as many as you like.

Important

During the Beta phase, we have removed the upper limit on the team size for the Small plan. When the Beta phase ends, that plan will be limited to a team size of two people.
